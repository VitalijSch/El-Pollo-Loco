In the dusty expanses of the Wild West lay a remote farm, its peaceful tranquility threatened by an unusual problem: Giant chickens, inexplicably grown to monstrous sizes, were terrorizing the surroundings.

Pepe Peligroso, an adventurous Mariachi with a passion for spicy sauce, heard of the danger and decided to come to the farm's aid. Armed with his legendary fiery salsa, he set out on his journey.

Upon reaching the farm, he witnessed the extent of the threat: A massive chicken with sharp claws and a wild gaze menaced the peace of the farm. Pepe wasted no time and began distributing his spicy sauce to unsettle the giant fowl.

With a skillful throw of his fiery sauce, Pepe managed to agitate and put the giant chicken to flight. The other chickens, taken aback by the unexpected distraction, followed their leader and vanished into the expanses of the prairie.

To progress in the game, the chickens must be defeated. The player can either jump on them or throw bottles to kill them. The Big Chicken can only be defeated by throwing bottles at him.
